Here is a description of a website dedicated to helping expatriates find housing without a middleman:

Bio:

Welcome to mughtarib, a website that aims to facilitate the process of searching for housing for expatriates without the need for intermediaries. We provide you with a comprehensive platform that combines direct offers from landlords and tenants, ensuring fair prices and hassle-free experiences.

Goals:

  • Advanced Search: Use our advanced search engine to find apartments and houses that fit your needs, with filter options including location, price, number of rooms, and more.
  • Direct Viewings: Use our advanced View rental listings directly from landlords, giving you the opportunity to negotiate directly without any additional commissions.
  • Residential Guide: We provide comprehensive tips and guides on how to move, the culture of living in the new country, and the best areas to live in.
  • Reviews and Opinions: Read reviews and opinions from previous tenants about properties and landlords to ensure you make the right decision.
  • Continuous Support: Our support team is available to assist with any questions or issues you may have during the search or rental process.
